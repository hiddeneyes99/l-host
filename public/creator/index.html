Behind The Build

One day I had a 4 GB private file on my phone that needed to move to my PC — fast. WhatsApp: 2 GB limit. Telegram: 2 GB limit, painfully slow. Both routing through their servers.

I had no real option. No tool that was truly local, truly unlimited, truly fast. So instead of compromising — I built one myself.

What started as a personal tool to solve one problem became a full-featured private media vault used across Android, Linux, Windows, and macOS.
