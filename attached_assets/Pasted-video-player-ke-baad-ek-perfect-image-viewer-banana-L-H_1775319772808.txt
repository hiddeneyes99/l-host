video player ke baad ek 'perfect' image viewer banana L-Host ke liye agla bada step hai. Images visual content hoti hain, aur unhe explore karna utna hi intuitive aur enjoyable hona chahiye jitna video dekhna. Netflix jaisi premium feel agar videos ke liye hai, toh images ke liye hum Google Photos ya Apple Photos jaisi fluidity aur smart features integrate kar sakte hain.

Chaliye dekhte hain ki ek professional aur features-packed image viewer mein kya kya important hota hai, taaki user ko media explore karne mein sach mein maza aaye:

1. Seamless Navigation & Viewing
Previous/Next Transitions: Ekdam smooth animations ke saath images swipe ya nav buttons se change honi chahiye. Ek bhi lag nahi hona chahiye.

Intuitive Zoom & Pan: Pinch-to-zoom (mobile) ya smooth scroll wheel/slider zoom (desktop). Zoom karne par high-resolution detail flawless dikhni chahiye, bina image blocky hue.

Smooth Rotation: Image ko left ya right smoothly rotate karne ka option, automated seamless transitions ke saath.

2. Smart Information & Organization
Detailed Metadata Display: Ek dedicated button/icon jo click karne par image ki saari info (title, file size, resolution, file type, EXIF data jaise date, camera, safe location approx.) ek clean, readable format mein (monospace font preferred in dark theme) display kare.

Smart Categorization: Backend (scanner.py) se integrate karke images ko date ya category wise view karne ka option (Google Photos style), lekin L-Host scope mein abhi hum single viewer interface features pe focus karenge.

3. Interaction & Engagement
Simple Visual Filters: User experience ko engage karne ke liye kuch basic non-destructive visual filters smoothly apply karne ka option (jaise Grayscale, Sepia, Reset).

Gesture Visualization: Mobile user experience ko focus karte hue, hum subtle visuals (jaise glowing traces) implement kar sakte hain jo visual feedback dein swipe gestures ke liye, navigation ke time.

One-Click Actions: Download aur Share buttons (simple links) easily accessible hone chahiye.

4. Aesthetics & Engagement
Responsive & Consistent Design: L-Host ki high-tech glowing dark theme, rounded corners aur Cyan/Teal accents ko maintain karte hue... mobile se desktop tak perfect responsiveness.

Micro-interactions: Har interaction (click, toggle, navigation) par subtle animations aur glowing feedback pulses, bilkul premium app jaisa feel dene ke liye.
